Welcome to the Expert Exercises



This pages contains exercise that is or should be done if and only if you already have foundation in the muscle areas you want to work on. This kinds of exercises are not what the beginner should do as instead of gain you might have pain instead. This workouts or exercise routines are for those who have already have foundation or quite strong muscle and is wanting to tone his/her body more or increase the intensity for more fat burning exercises.


Be careful when doing this kind of exercises when one doesn't have experience in exercising as you may get injuries instead if you just imitate the exercise blindly. One must atleast have knowledge in working out.
