Missing Arm Hold

Missing Arm Hold Exercise is good for working out core muscle. Because it works your one arm is being forced to hold up the weight of your body. And your core muscles are being utilized in order to keep your body parallel with the floor.


How do you do the missing arm hold?


Get in a normal pushup position. Lift one arm of the ground and place it behind your back. Hold this position. Repeat on the other side.
