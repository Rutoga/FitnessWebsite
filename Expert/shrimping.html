Shrimping Exercise

Shrimping exercise is a move used by many judo martial artists. The move is also used by jujitsu fighters. Itâ€™s a great exercise to focus on strengthening muscles used when an opponent is pinning you down to the mat.


How do you do the shrimping?


Laying on the ground in the fetal position roll to the other side using your feet. Your body should look like a shrimp out of water. You should slowly be working your way across the room. Once you get to the other side you can turn around and shrimp back across the room.

https://youtu.be/YeZngOPVBWY

Area of effects

This exercise is a whole body work out.


Benefits of doing shrimping

Shrimping is more helpful to martial artist as it helps the body get used to squirming out from being pinned. The more you do an exercise the more your body will get used to it. For martial artists this is important for body memory because it will be easier to do when you need it. As for the normal people, it helps greatly for fat burning as this exercise works out the whole body. It is good for muscle and bones also flexibility.
