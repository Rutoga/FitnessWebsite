V Up

V up sit up is a classic exercise to strengthen your abs. The exercise is beloved by fitness fanatics for its ability to work the upper and lower abdominal muscles simultaneously. The exercise demands flexibility as well as caution.


How do you do the V Up?


Lay face up on the ground with your legs extended and arms above your head. Simultaneously lift your arms and upper body along with your lower legs (knees straight) up until they meet above you. Lower your body and legs back to starting position. Repeat.

Benefits of doing V Up

V-ups target the abdominal muscles, tone down the obliques, strengthen your back muscles and challenge the entire core. Moreover, it works on your quadriceps and hamstrings at the same time. It's good for working out 6 pack abs.
